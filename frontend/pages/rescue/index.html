🧠 How AI Analysis Works

  • Your description is scanned for emergency keywords
  • Incident type and severity are automatically classified
  • The best-suited rescue team is recommended
  • An estimated response time is calculated
  • Decision policy is applied instantly

⚡ Decision Policy

  • Critical → Auto-dispatched immediately. Control room notified.
  • High / Medium → Sent for supervisor approval before dispatch.
  • Low → Logged and placed in pending review queue.

🚒 Rescue Teams Available

  • Fire Response Unit – Fires, explosions
  • Flood Rescue (NDRF) – Floods, drowning
  • SDRF Structural Team – Collapses, earthquakes
  • Hazmat Unit – Gas leaks, chemical incidents
  • Emergency Response Team – Accidents, injuries
  • Electrical Emergency Unit – Live wires, electrocution
  • Civic Emergency Team – Fallen trees, debris
